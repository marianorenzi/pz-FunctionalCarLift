title=Functional Car Lift
description=[h2]How does it work?[/h2]
description=Makes the car shop lifts functional, removing the need for a car jack when the car is near the lift. There is no obvious effect besides the "car jack" item being removed from the list of "Required tools" on the "Vehicle Mechanics" window.
description=
description=[h2]Move the lift to your own car shop![/h2]
description=Enabling the option to convert the vanilla tiles, you will be able to pick-up the world car lifts and place them on your own base. It requires a screwdriver and Electrical Level 3, and will result in four heavy parts. Be careful, it can break! Improve your chances with more Electrical level.
description=[i](Mechanical or Metalworking requirements were not possible with how moveable tiles work.)[/i]
description=
description=[h2]Sandbox options[/h2]
description=- [u][b]Range:[/b][/u] The distance range from the vehicle where the car lift operates (radius from the driver door tile).
description=- [u][b]Require Power:[/b][/u] Indicates if the car lift requires available power to operate, whether it is from hydro (world power) or a generator.
description=- [u][b]Convert to Movable Tiles:[/b][/u] Convert vanilla tiles into moveable tiles. This process can be undone with the revert option.
description=- [u][b]Revert to Vanilla Tiles:[/b][/u] Revert moveable car lift tiles into vanilla tiles. To succesfully revert, this option must be kept enabled until ALL tiles are reverted.
description=
description=[h2]Frequently Asked Questions[/h2]
description=[b]- Can I install it to an existing save?[/b]
description=- Yes, by default the mod just makes the existing lift tiles on the map functional. If you want to craft them, you will have less chances to find a magazine since the loot spawns at map discovery.
description=
description=[b]- How do I learn the recipe?[/b]
description=- Find and read the "Carzone Commodities: Car Lift" with Mechanics 4 and Metalworking 3, or learn the recipe automatically at Mechanics 6 and Metalworking 4.
description=
description=[b]- How should I place the lift tiles?[/b]
description=- The tiles must be placed in the form of a complete lift with the correct orientation, with both columns and center pieces. It doesn't matter how many center pieces to place, it could be 1 or 10 (for wide vehicles).
description=
description=[b]- How can I pick lift?[/b]
description=- You just pick the individual pieces as you would any furniture (if you meet the skill and tool requirements). To be able to pick vanilla car lifts (the ones that spawn at car shops), you need to enable the "Convert to Movable Tiles" Sandbox option. This will convert the vanilla tiles into the mod tiles (the same you can craft) as they are discovered. Be mindful, there is a chance to break the pieces that will decrease with better Mechanics level.
description=
description=[b]- Why does my vehicle don't fit in the lift?[/b]
description=- Some vanilla car lift on small repair shops have only 2 tiles without hitboxes, so the fit is TIGHT. Big repair shops' lifts are wider (3 tiles without hitboxes), and you can build your own custom width car lift. Some modded cars also are very tall, and collide with the top bars.
